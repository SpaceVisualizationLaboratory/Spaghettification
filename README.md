Spaghettification
=================
<img src=https://raw.githubusercontent.com/SpaceVisualizationLaboratory/Images/master/Spagettify.jpg>
A simulation of the astrophysical phenomenon called “spaghettification”, using a kinect for input.
The exhibit was originally featured in the Space Visualization Laboratory (SVL) during the "Galactic Gastronomy"-themed Adler After Dark event at the Adler Planetarium on 20 November 2014.  It continues to be available for display in the SVL. A video of the exhibit is here[NEEDS LINK].
